I'm a quantitative biologist using ML to design better RNA therapeutics. 

I earned my PhD in the Quake lab at Stanford. My focus was on systems biology. In particular, I generated data and frameworks to model the dynamics of the immune system. Working models of these dynamics would allow us to use the immune system to solve an array of problems including cancer, aging, and auto-immune disease.
