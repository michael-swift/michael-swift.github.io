I'm a quantitative biologist using ML to design better RNA therapeutics. 

I earned my PhD in the Quake lab at Stanford. My focus was on systems biology. In particular, I generated data and frameworks to model the dynamics of the immune system. Models of these dynamics would enable us use the human immune system as a means to solve an array of problems including cancer, aging, and auto-immune disease.
